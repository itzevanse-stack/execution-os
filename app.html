The Grand Slam Offer Formula (Alex Hormozi)
Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood × Speed ÷ Effort & Sacrifice = Offer Value
The bigger the dream, the higher the perceived likelihood, the faster the result, and the less effort required — the more irresistible your offer becomes.
🎯
Dream Outcome
What transformation does your client get? Make it vivid, specific, and measurable.
📊
Perceived Likelihood
Why should they believe it will work for THEM? Proof, case studies, your track record.
⚡
Speed
How fast will they see results? Faster = more valuable. Always include a clear timeline.
🚀
Effort & Sacrifice
What do they NOT have to do? Remove friction. Make it easy. Add done-for-you elements.
